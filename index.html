Feminine Calendars

Given the world situation and that many users do not trust that the apps keep their privacy, I decided to share the printable we had in our feminine agendas when I was a teenager and smartphones were not a thing. They are painless to follow, and you can keep them wherever you want: in your diary, organizer or bathroom. And considering that most apps use mainly pink... well pink, if used, will be just in small amounts.
How to use: circle or mark the days corresponding to your period, and if you have the information add the last few months as well. As the days are in columns, you will see the pattern of your period and can predict the next arrival.
